Tilt Gather: Moving the particles in a polyomino onto the same field

After the particles/robots have successfully completed the mission, you want to gather all particles. If the polyomino is connected at there is at least one wall, one can show that this is always possible if we are able to make precise one field movements and particles are allowed to overlap.
